Residential

Unlock the potential of your residential real estate with expert investment, financing, valuation, due diligence and design strategies.

Multifamily


We design multi-family homes, which are single structures partitioned to enable more than one family living separately. They can range from a duplex (two houses in one building) to homes or small apartment buildings with up to four distinct apartments.

Affordable Housing


We build cost-effective housing so that families can afford it and still have money for other requirements like food, transportation, and medical care. Thus, what is "affordable" is determined by the income of a household.

Student Housing


We construct a residential unit owned by a participating nonprofit entity and placed on real land owned by that entity for use by an individual enrolled in a public college, public university, or participating private college.

Senior Housing/Assisted Living


We construct a unique blend of housing, individualized supportive services, and health-related care to meet the unique needs of persons who require assistance with daily living tasks.
